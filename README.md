# CourierMap

**A driver-only shop rating map for rideshare and delivery couriers.**

CourierMap is a map app with a shop rating system that **only verified drivers** can
contribute to — separate from Google Maps. The idea is simple: before you commit to a
pickup, you can see what other drivers have said about a shop, so you can decide whether
to **wait or cancel** based on real history instead of guessing.

This is especially useful when you're new to an area and don't yet know which shops are
reliable and which ones will leave you waiting.

---

## Why CourierMap?

When you arrive at a new shop, you can pull up CourierMap and instantly see:

- ⭐ **Wait-time ratings & comments** left by other drivers — so if a place is known for
  long waits, you can cancel and protect a quest or hourly target instead of getting stuck.
- 📍 **Pickup tips** — driver notes on navigating tricky locations: multi-level malls,
  hidden takeaway-only spots, where to actually park, which entrance to use.
- 🗺️ **Offline-first map** — pan and zoom without burning data; the map keeps working
  even with a weak signal.

It's the kind of local knowledge drivers usually trade in group chats — collected in one
place, pinned to the actual shop on a map.

---

## Who it's for

Rideshare and delivery drivers (Uber, DoorDash, Menulog, and others) who want to make
faster, smarter decisions at pickup. Ratings are contributed by drivers, for drivers.
